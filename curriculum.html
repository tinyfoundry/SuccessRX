The Foundations Learning Path

A prescriptive, 5-step curriculum designed to turn support agents into strategic CSMs.

Step 01

The Strategic Foundation

Objective: Transition mindset from reactive support to proactive value creation.

  • Defining the "Customer Success" mandate within a SaaS context.
  • Understanding the revenue impact of retention vs. acquisition.
  • Setting boundaries: When to say "no" to the customer.
  • Defining your "Why": Personal and professional alignment.
Step 02

Lifecycle Value Stream

Objective: Map the customer journey from Onboarding to Renewal.

  • Building a "Time-to-Value" roadmap.
  • Designing playbooks for the Onboarding "Danger Zone."
  • Creating QBRs (Quarterly Business Reviews) as checkpoints.
  • Automating lifecycle touchpoints without losing the human touch.
Step 03

Vital Signs & Data

Objective: Master Health Scoring and identify leading indicators of churn.

  • Leading vs. Lagging Indicators.
  • Building a weighted Health Scorecard.
  • Identifying "Infection Points" (Red Flags).
Step 04

High-Impact EBRs

Objective: Architect the perfect Executive Business Review (EBR).

  • Stakeholder Mapping: Who has the power to cancel?
  • Structuring the Slide Deck: Context, Insight, Roadmap.
  • Calculating and communicating ROI.
Step 05

Growth Mechanics

Objective: Secure renewals and identify organic upsell opportunities.

  • The Art of the Renewal: Making it a "done deal."
  • Leveraging NPS for Referral Generation.
  • Identifying "White Space" for cross-selling.
