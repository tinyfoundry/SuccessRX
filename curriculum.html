The Foundations Learning Path

A prescriptive, 5-step curriculum designed to turn support agents into strategic CSMs.

Start Foundations Training

01. The Strategic Foundation

Baseline Health
▼

Objective: Transition mindset from reactive support to proactive value creation.

In this opening module, we diagnose the "baseline health" of your current role. We establish the difference between Customer Service and Customer Success.


Key Lessons:
  • Defining the "Customer Success" mandate within a SaaS context.
  • Understanding the revenue impact of retention vs. acquisition.
  • Setting boundaries: When to say "no" to the customer.
  • Defining your "Why": Personal and professional alignment.

02. Lifecycle Value Stream

Mapping the Journey
▼

Objective: Map the customer journey from Onboarding to Renewal.

Just as a doctor tracks a patient's recovery, the CSM must track the Lifecycle. We dissect the critical phases: Implementation (Intake), Adoption (Treatment), and Value Realization (Recovery).


Key Lessons:
  • Building a "Time-to-Value" roadmap.
  • Designing playbooks for the Onboarding "Danger Zone."
  • Creating QBRs (Quarterly Business Reviews) as checkpoints.
  • Automating lifecycle touchpoints without losing the human touch.

03. Vital Signs & Data

Data Diagnostics
▼

Objective: Master Health Scoring and identify leading indicators of churn.

Stop guessing. We teach you how to read the "Vital Signs" of your account data. This module turns raw usage data into actionable intelligence.


Key Lessons:
  • Leading vs. Lagging Indicators.
  • Building a weighted Health Scorecard.
  • Identifying "Infection Points" (Red Flags).
  • Using tools like Gainsight or Salesforce for telemetry.

04. High-Impact EBRs

Executive Communication
▼

Objective: Architect the perfect Executive Business Review (EBR).

Prescribing the right solution to the wrong person fails. This module focuses on multi-threaded relationships and C-Suite communication.


Key Lessons:
  • Stakeholder Mapping: Who has the power to cancel?
  • Structuring the Slide Deck: Context, Insight, Roadmap.
  • Calculating and communicating ROI.
  • Handling "The Trap": When executives want a discount to renew.

05. Growth Mechanics

Expansion & Advocacy
▼

Objective: Secure renewals and identify organic upsell opportunities.

The best outcome is a full recovery and renewed vitality. We cover how to identify expansion opportunities naturally, rather than forcing a sales pitch.


Key Lessons:
  • The Art of the Renewal: Making it a "done deal," not a negotiation.
  • Leveraging NPS for Referral Generation.
  • Identifying "White Space" for cross-selling.
  • Creating a "Customer Advisory Board" loop.
